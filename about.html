What is Habit Tracker?

Habit Tracker is a web application designed to help users track their daily habits and monitor their progress over time.

How does it work?

Simply create habits you want to track, mark them as completed each day, and visualize your habit streaks. Habit Tracker provides a motivational tool for building and maintaining healthy habits.

Why use Habit Tracker?

Tracking your habits can lead to increased productivity, improved well-being, and overall better quality of life. Habit Tracker makes it easy to stay accountable and achieve your goals.
